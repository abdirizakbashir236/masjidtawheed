About the Masjid

A waqf for the worship of Allah and the spread of authentic Islam.

Masjid Tawheed is a 501(c)(3) Islamic waqf serving the Muslims of metro Atlanta. The masjid is exclusively a place of worship and a center created to educate and spread the true message of Islam — adopting a middle course in all its affairs, rooted in the Qur'an, the Sunnah, and the understanding of the early generations of Islam.

As a community waqf, no specific group, ethnicity, or individual lays claim to ownership of the masjid. It operates under a collective governance model — no single individual controls the masjid's accounts or property — so that it remains a trust for all Muslims, in perpetuity.

Our Methodology

Manhaj As-Salafi

The Prophet (ﷺ) said: “My Nation will split into 73 Sects. All of Them are in The Fire Except One. Which is it O Messenger of Allah? He Said (ﷺ): Those who are Upon What I am Upon Today and My Companions.”

This is Manhaj As-Salafi, and it is the path to salvation, leading to Paradise. There is no other way; all other paths are misguided.

The Prophet, peace and blessings be upon him, was wary of these Misguided Callers [Ahlu Bid'ah] who want to turn people away from the path of the Salaf, and he informed us that they are callers at the gates of Hellfire; whoever obeys them, they will throw him into it.

— Shaikh Salih Al-Fawzan [حفظه الله] — Member of the Council of Senior Scholars of Saudi Arabia
